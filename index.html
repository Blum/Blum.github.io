Iliyana Trifonova

Senior Research Associate, School of Psychology


Hello, my name is Iliyana Trifonova and I'm currently an NRSA postdoc fellow in Timothy Brady's lab at the University of California, San Diego. In Spring 2024, I'll be joining the Behavioral Science group at the University of Warwick's Psychology Department as an Assistant Professor.

Download my CV in PDF

My primary research interest is in combining empirical work and formal modeling to study attention, memory and decision-making. I'm also interested in topics on best practices in measurement and theory assessment in psychology.

I received my PhD in Psychology in 2019 at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. While at Illinois, I completed my dissertation project under the supervision of Aaron Benjamin and collaborated with David Irwin as well as Michel Regenwetter.
